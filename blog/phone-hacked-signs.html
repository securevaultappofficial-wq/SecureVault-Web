How to Know If Your Phone Is Hacked

If your phone feels strange, do not panic first. Look for patterns. One small issue may be normal, but several signs together can mean malware, a risky app, or account compromise.

Common warning signs

  • Battery drains much faster than usual even when the phone is idle.
  • Unknown apps appear, especially apps installed outside the Play Store.
  • Popups, redirects, or fake security warnings appear often.
  • Data usage increases without a clear reason.
  • Banking, email, or social accounts show login alerts you did not create.

What to do first

Disconnect from risky Wi-Fi, uninstall unknown apps, update Android and Play Protect, change important passwords from a clean device, and enable two-factor authentication. If money is involved in India, call 1930 and report at cybercrime.gov.in quickly.
