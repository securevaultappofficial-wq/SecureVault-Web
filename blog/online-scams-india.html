Top Online Scams in India and How to Stay Safe

Many scams are not technical at first. They use fear, urgency, greed, or confusion. The safest response is to slow down, verify, and never share secret codes.

Common scams to watch

  • UPI fraud and fake payment confirmation screenshots.
  • OTP scam calls pretending to be bank or delivery support.
  • KYC update messages with fake forms and APK links.
  • Digital arrest scam calls using fear and fake authority.
  • Fake job, fake loan, and investment WhatsApp group scams.

Safety rules

Never share OTP, UPI PIN, card CVV, or passwords. Do not install apps from links sent by strangers. If fraud happens, call 1930 quickly and report the complaint on the official cybercrime portal.
