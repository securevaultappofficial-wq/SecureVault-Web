How to Check If a WhatsApp Link Is Safe

Scam links often create urgency: blocked account, unpaid bill, KYC update, reward, courier issue, or police warning. The goal is to make you tap before thinking.

Before opening a link

  • Check whether the domain name is spelled correctly.
  • Be careful with shortened URLs that hide the final destination.
  • Do not enter OTP, UPI PIN, card details, or passwords from a chat link.
  • Look for pressure words like urgent, final warning, account blocked, or immediate arrest.
  • Search the company website manually instead of trusting the forwarded link.

Common scam examples

Fake bank messages, fake electricity bills, fake courier delivery links, investment groups, job application traps, and fake government forms are common ways attackers collect private data.
