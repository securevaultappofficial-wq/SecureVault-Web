How an AI Security Adviser Helps You Understand Threats

Security reports often use technical words like phishing, malware, spyware, suspicious permissions, or risky domains. An AI security adviser can translate those warnings into clear next steps.

What users can ask

  • Is this message a scam?
  • Is this link safe to open?
  • Why is this APK risky?
  • What does my scan report mean?
  • What should I do if my phone may be hacked?

Why this matters

Fast, simple explanations reduce panic and help users avoid dangerous actions like sharing OTPs, installing unknown apps, or calling fake support numbers.
